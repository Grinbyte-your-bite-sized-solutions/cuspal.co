Why We Started Cuspal

As healthcare professionals who transitioned into technology, we witnessed firsthand the gap between groundbreaking research and real-world implementation. Too often, brilliant discoveries remain trapped in academic papers, never reaching the patients who could benefit from them.

We founded Cuspal to bridge this gap. Our unique combination of clinical expertise, technical skills, and deep understanding of healthcare workflows allows us to transform research into practical, deployable solutions that make a real difference in patient care.

Every member of our founding team has walked in the shoes of healthcare providers. We understand the challenges, the constraints, and the immense potential for technology to amplify human capabilities in healthcare. This is why we're not just building software – we're crafting solutions that truly serve both providers and patients.
